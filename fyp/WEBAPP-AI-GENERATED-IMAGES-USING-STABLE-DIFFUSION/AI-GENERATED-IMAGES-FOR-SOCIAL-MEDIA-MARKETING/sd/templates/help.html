How App Works

Functionality 1: Image from Image (Free Model) (512*512px)

Upload an image, and our free model will generate a modified version based on the input. Strenth is how much you want to change the current image. Unconditional prompt is what you don't want in the generated image

Functionality 2: Text to Image (Free Model) (512*512px)

Convert text into images using our free model. Simply input text, and the model will generate an image representing the provided text. Unconditional prompt is what you don't want in the generated image

Functionality 3: Text to Image (OpenAI - Paid) (1024*1024px)

Similar to the free text-to-image feature, but this utilizes a paid model from OpenAI.

Functionality 4: Generate Captions for Social Media Post

Generate captions for your social media posts by analyzing the image you've generated. Our app will analyze the content of the generated image and suggest suitable captions for your posts.

Functionality 5: Edit Image and Download

Edit your generated images by applying various effects such as cropping, adding templates, rotating, adjusting brightness, and contrast. Once satisfied with the edits, you can download the edited image in your desired format (e.g., PNG or JPEG).
